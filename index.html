Jellifish gives simplicity and speed to anyone who wants to cook.

Jellifish tells you what you need to know, when you need to know it, and not much else.

Guiding novice cooks, experienced chefs, first time feast makers...and anyone else who wants to fix a meal through delicious recipes

Helping first time cooks make five star meals like those of experienced chefs without the headache

All you need to know, when you need it. Don’t worry about when to check on the water or turn off the oven; Jellifish will let you know!
